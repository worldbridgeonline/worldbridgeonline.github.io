Mathew Lawrence

Founder and Managing Partner


35+ years of global language translation, localization and teaching experience out of which 13+ years living in the US, crafting coaching modules in various languages such as Spanish, Portuguese for universities and schools; including University of Tennessee, West Virginia University, Montverde Academy (Florida) and Gilman School (Baltimore). Extensive experience in streamlining, design and coordination of teaching content to avoid syllabus redundancy, digitization of educational material; use of educational software and online tools, Course mapping for Advanced Spanish Culture courses.
