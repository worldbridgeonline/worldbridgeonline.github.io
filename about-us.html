Our Company


Our journey began in the year 1981, when CA Dhirendra Sangoi commenced providing Chartered Accountancy services under the banner ‘D.P. Sangoi & Co.’. As the practice grew, we shifted base to our Dadar (E) office, inducted new partners and today we stand as full-fledged Chartered Accountancy firm having 5 partners and a team of 20 skilled personnel offering full spectrum of tax, assurance and consultancy services. Over the years, we have added several dimensions to our practice in terms of increase in clientele, quantum of work, practice areas and work force.

Soon after incorporation, the practice leapfrogged from a small to a mid-sized consultancy firm and built a base of loyal clients seeking the quality of services and keen eye for detail, Mr. Sangoi was providing. This laid the path for the next generation of Partners to step-in and today we stand as a one-stop business solution, representing a diversified portfolio of clientele ranging from High net worth individuals to corporate businesses across different industries.

Our growth story has always been hand-in-hand with our mission to render professional services of the highest standard to clients, upholding the code of conduct and ethics of the profession. We believe that high client satisfaction coupled with consummation of our core mission has been our real achievement.

Charging a client merely based on time spent isn’t our taste, there has to be a correlation between the fees charged and the value of the work done. Time costing rewards inefficiency and tempts professionals to behave unethically. From the client's perspective, it is uncontrollable and does nothing more efficiently than generate unpleasant surprises.

We believe in competitive pricing, striking the right balance between quality of services, we provide, and the professional fees, we charge.
